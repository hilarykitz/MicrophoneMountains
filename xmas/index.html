The Great Christmas Migration

The team here at GoEuro might not be able to sort out your wish-list for Santa or take care of Christmas dinner, but we can help out with your travel plans.

Along with being a transport search engine comparing coach, train and flights, we are also an international company, where many of us will be travelling to spend time with loved ones over December.

Therefore, we wanted to prepare ourselves by creating an infographic to understand one of the busiest travel periods of the year, or as we like to call ‘The Great Christmas Migration’ in attempt to assess travel and retail trends during the country’s biggest holiday.


Using historical travel data, combined with GoEuro search engine data and our GoEuro poll findings, we projected the forecast within our infographic to display this season's migration exodus.
